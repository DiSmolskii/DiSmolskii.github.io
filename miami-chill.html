Miami Chill

IOS & Android Application
01. Project Information.

About Project.

Overview

Miami Chill is one of the music albums in the Music Box game, suitable for anyone who wants to enjoy music and have fun. If you want to feel like a real DJ, try mixing beats and unleash your imagination.

How does it work?

In each gaming session, you can see 7 people waiting to make a sound. At the bottom of the screen, you can find 20 different colorful elements. Hold the item icon and drag it onto a random person. This person will acquire a specific outfit and make a specific sound.

06. Motion Design

Сreating a beautiful animations

Every album in the Music Box game is made in a unique style, and the Miami Chill album is no exception. The album includes 30 animations and 3 video clips.

Each animation is created in Lottie format, which offers significant advantages: a 10-fold increase in loading speed, high quality, interactivity, and scalability.

Character animations had to be synchronized with unique sounds, so we separately animated facial expressions and various body parts in time with the music.

For a more enjoyable gaming experience, we created interactive clips that you can watch by selecting the desired set of items and pressing the "Combo" button.

Design in AppStore.

We have prepared visual resources such as screenshots, icon design, promo videos, and other promotional materials. As thousands of apps are released daily, standing out from the crowd is important. In doing so, we still remember App Store recommendations.
